POKEMONS AND THEIR EVOLUTIONS

FROAKIE

Froakie is a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation VI and is the Water-type Starter of Kalos.

Evolutions

  • FROGADIER
  • GRENINJA

CHARMANDER

Charmander, known as Hitokage in Japan, is a Pokémon species in Nintendo's and Game Freak's Pokémon franchise.

Evolutions

  • CHARMELEON
  • CHARIZARD

BULBASAUR

Bulbasaur is a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon species in Nintendo and Game Freak's Pokémon franchise.

Evolutions

  • IVYSAUR
  • VENUSAUR

SQUIRTLE

Squirtle, known as Zenigame in Japan, is a Pokémon species in Nintendo and Game Freak's Pokémon franchise. It was designed by Atsuko Nishida. Its name was changed from Zenigame to Squirtle during the English localization of the series in order to give it a "clever and descriptive name."

Evolutions

  • WARTORTLE
  • BLASTOISE

PIKACHU

Pikachu is a fictional species in the Pokémon media franchise.

Evolutions

  • PICHU
  • RAICHU

FENNEKIN

Fennekin is a Fire-type Pokémon introduced in Generation VI. It is the Fire-type Starter of Kalos.

Evolutions

  • BRAIXEN
  • DELPHOX

SNORUNT

Snorunt is an Ice-type Pokémon introduced in Generation III.

Evolutions

  • GLALIE
  • FLOSLASS

CHESPIN

Chespin (Japanese: ハリマロン Harimaron) is a Grass-type Pokémon introduced in Generation VI.

Evolutions

  • QUILLADIN
  • CHESNAUGHT

SCYTHER

Scyther is a Bug/Flying-type Pokémon introduced in Generation.

Evolutions

  • SCIZOR
  • KLEAVOR

JIGGLYBUFF

Jigglypuff, known in Japan as Purin, is a Pokémon species.

Evolutions

  • IGYBUFF
  • WIGGLYBUFF
